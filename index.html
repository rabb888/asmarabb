Story of Rabb Businesses

From Humble Beginnings to Business Excellence

Founded in 2025 by visionary entrepreneur Mohammed Rabb, Rabb Businesses started as a small consultancy firm in Dubai with just two employees. What began as a passion for helping local businesses optimize their operations has grown into a comprehensive business transformation partner serving clients across the UAE.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. From our first client - a local retail store looking to improve its inventory management - to our current portfolio of multinational corporations and government entities, we've maintained the same personal touch and dedication to excellence.

Milestones in Our Journey

  • 2025 Recognized as "Top Emerging Consultancy Firm" by UAE Business Awards
  • 2025 Expanded team to 50+ consultants serving 200+ clients annually

Today, Rabb Businesses stands as a testament to the power of vision, perseverance, and client-focused service. Our story continues to unfold as we help shape the future of business in the UAE and beyond.
